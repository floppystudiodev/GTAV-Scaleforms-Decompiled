2. Work-Spirituality Balance.Do you think I’ve ever worked 9 to 5? Not in this time zone! A true entrepreneur works two hours a day max. Any more than that, you’re over-thinking it and not dedicating enough time to self-improvement. An old leper in Nepal once hissed at me, “A man cannot rule over his domain until he’s first learned how to worship himself.” I never feel stress or self-doubt because I know I’m delivering on my potential holistically.
