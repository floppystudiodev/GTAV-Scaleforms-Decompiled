When shy law school dropout Will Longhorn is bequeathed a million dollars by a woman he had an affair with as a teenager, he can’t believe his luck… until he gets accused of committing her murder and is forced to go on the run to Australia. He soon realizes this is a terrible idea as the place is full of savages that annoy everyone. He falls in love with a young runaway called Molly who teaches him to believe in himself. Yet her accent is so annoying he kills her.When he’s arrested and tried in court for first-degree murder, there is a 40-minute court case sequence, in which Will is repeatedly asked by the Judge, “Aye mate, why did ya do it?  Crikey!”He escapes and rows back to civilization.
